  • Custom Web Design

  • E-Commerce Solutions

2. How much do you charge?

The cost of a WordPress website can vary widely based on the complexity and specific requirements of the project. For the Project-based, basic websites starts at Php 25,000. Redesign websites starts at Php 15,000 while more complex projects like e-commerce sites are priced higher. Please contact us for a detailed quote.


3. How long will it take to build my website?

The timeline for a WordPress website project depends on the scope and complexity of the work. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 6-8 weeks or longer. We will provide a more accurate timeline after discussing your specific requirements..


4. Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure your website remains updated, secure, and performing well. This includes regular updates, backups, security monitoring, and performance optimization.


5. What is your payment structure?

Our payment structure usually includes a 50% upfront deposit before starting the project, with the remaining balance due upon project completion.
